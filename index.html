It is made up of sound control and live projections department, the photography department, the video and live streaming department. It is the media desire to influence the masses for Christ through the creative thinking and design process of effective communication. The Youth Ministry is a ministry under Christ Apostolic Church International. It has its age span between 16years -45years. All well members that falls within the category of the above stated age group is considered to be under the youth ministry. The core mandate of the youth ministry is to help mobilize members grow in the Lord Spiritually and also help develop them for the future of the church.

It is one of the departments/wings within the church (CACI-FWC, Kent City Assembly) responsible for managing, monitoring and supervising all activities in the church. It serves as the technical eye to the church. This is was formed by the management of the church with open-access participation from all members of the church. The ministry is headed by Dr. Mrs. Bridgetta Donkor. Members of the church are always welcomed to this group but acceptance will be reviewed by members of the ministry. Core mandate is to perform different roles in the church that is; to assist with the church activities (before, within and after church activities).
